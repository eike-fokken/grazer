◆ after_state_index

int Model::Networkproblem::Statecomponent::after_state_index {-1}
private

The first index after start_state_index, that is not "owned" by this Equationcomponent.

Most equation components write only to their own indices between start_state_index (inclusive) and after_state_index (exclusive). There are exceptions though, e.g. instances of Gasnode.
