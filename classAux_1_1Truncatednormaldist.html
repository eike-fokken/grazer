Detailed Description

provides a truncated normal distribution sample on invocation.

This uses a simple accept-reject algorithm. It should be highly inefficient, if the probability of obtaining a sample of the usual normal distribution within the interval [lower_bound,upper_bound] is small. Also not thread safe, see under Normaldistribution.
